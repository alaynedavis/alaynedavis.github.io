PACKAGE REDESIGN

The objective of this project was to find a piece of packaging from the store that was designed poorly and redesign it. This redesign focused fully on visual design and aesthetic, not the functionality of the packaging. The packaging I chose to redesign was a box of lavender chamomile tea bags from the brand Bigelow. To achieve this redesign, I used Adobe Illustrator to design each face of the box. Then, I found a premade photoshop mock up, and I put my designs on the mockup to display them realistically.
